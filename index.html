FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver and set up the tents?
Yes! We handle full delivery, professional installation and takedown. Our team ensures everything is set up before your event.
Do you offer payment after delivery?
Yes, we offer payment after delivery options. Contact us to discuss terms for your specific event.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking 2–3 weeks in advance for large events. However, we accommodate last-minute requests where possible.
Are your tents weatherproof?
Yes, all our tents are made from high quality, weather-resistant materials that protect against rain, wind and strong sun.
Do you offer custom designs?
Absolutely! Custom colors, branding and design options available. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
What areas do you serve?
We serve clients across Kenya. Contact us with your location for delivery details and any applicable transport charges.
