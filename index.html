FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver and set up the tents?
Yes! We handle full delivery, professional installation and takedown. Our team ensures everything is set up properly before your event begins.
Do you offer payment after delivery?
Yes, we offer payment after delivery options. Contact us to discuss terms and arrangements for your specific event.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially for large events and peak seasons. However, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests.
Are your tents weatherproof?
Yes, all our tents are made from high quality, weather-resistant materials that protect against rain, wind and strong sun.
Do you offer custom tent designs?
Absolutely! We offer custom color, branding and design options to match your event theme. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
What areas do you serve?
We serve clients across Kenya. Contact us with your location and we will provide delivery details and any applicable transport charges.
